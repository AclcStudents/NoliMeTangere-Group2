REPUBLIC ACT NO. 1425

AN ACT TO INCLUDE IN THE CURRICULA OF ALL PUBLIC AND PRIVATE SCHOOLS, COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES COURSES ON THE LIFE, WORKS AND WRITINGS OF JOSE RIZAL, PARTICULARLY HIS NOVELS NOLI ME TANGERE AND EL FILIBUSTERISMO, AUTHORIZING THE PRINTING AND DISTRIBUTION THEREOF, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES In previous years, students and teachers heavily relied on library books and periodicals for their studies on Noli Me Tangere. However, the emergence of Information Technology has significantly enhanced the accessibility and sharing of ideas among individuals with diverse perspectives worldwide. Survey results indicate a growing trend in students utilizing the Internet for research purposes. This website has been thoughtfully designed and created to aid students in understanding the vital role that Noli Me Tangere played in the development of the Filipino nation. The website offers an extensive collection of materials in English both Filipino languages. Moreover, it is freely accessible to everyone. The endorsement of this website by the Commission on Higher Education has contributed to the increase in the number of visitors. Continuously, this website is dedicated to gathering and updating information about Noli Me Tangere, aiming to provide an even better service to its users." This revised version maintains the original message while enhancing clarity and flow.

This website is self-sustaining, non-profit, and non-partisan, and everyone is invited to visit and link with our website.
